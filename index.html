Background

I am currently working toward an exciting career change! After spending over a decade working with my hands to fix broken things, I am now pursuing a path that will allow me to build things from scratch using my brain, my imagination, and my creativity.

I have spent almost fifteen years travelling the world and working as a Jet Engine Mechanic. Those fifteen years were intermingled with brief spurts of mini-retirements on my family farm (where I milked a lot of goats). Neither of those skill sets have anything to do, at all, with web development. Just being honest.

Goals

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Python

Of course, I would rather win the lottery, but if for some reason that strategy doesn't pan out, then I intend to work for a living. And if I have to work for a living I might as well love what I do. For that reason I am dedicating whatever spare time I have left after busy days spent scratching lottery tickets to gaining sellable skills in the world of technology.
